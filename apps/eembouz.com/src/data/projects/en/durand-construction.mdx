## Overview

__Durand Construction__ is a company based in
__Brazzaville__, active in the fields of building construction
and engineering. The showcase website was designed to present their
projects, services, and work philosophy in a clear and functional way.

## Design

The interface is built around a __modular layout__, centered on
a clean typography system and a visual rhythm inspired by architectural
design principles. The structure highlights the key sections — _Work_
, _About_, and _Blog_ — with a readable and balanced
hierarchy.

The website was developed using a __static architecture__,
favoring speed, security, and minimal maintenance.

![Durand Construction - Blog section](/project/durand/5.png)

## Content

The site showcases a selection of recent projects, the services offered, and
a __blog__ area dedicated to sharing articles about
architecture, sustainability, and educational insights related to the
profession.

The overall goal is to strengthen the company’s online presence and highlight
their expertise through clean and refined communication.

## Technologies

The project relies on several complementary technologies: Astro for static
site generation, ensuring speed and performance; TypeScript for more
reliable and maintainable code; CSS for visual structure and styling; and
Markdown for simple and efficient content management.
